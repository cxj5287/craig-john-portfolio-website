About
Craig John is a multimedia artist currently enrolled at the Pennyslvania State University, earning a Bachelor of Design in Digital Arts and Media Design. Interested in the expanding medium of 3D, his current focus is on creating engaging and immersive renders and animations in a three dimensional digital space. Recently, he has started to delve into web development on both the front and back-end, looking to design and code interactive user experiences on the web.

Currently living in Kennett Square, Pennsylvania, Craig's hobbies outside of work include hiking, playing music, and watching films.
